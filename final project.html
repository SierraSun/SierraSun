ScatterPlot：Average Vote of Movies

Database:

version 3 of The Movie Database (TMDb) API.

How it calculated:

Vote averages are calculated as an average across all ratings and ordered from highest to lowest by using "bayesian average". This method of calculating the average allows ratings to go up and down as they are rated as such, but also prevents a single vote from over taking the highest rated spot. It also prevents the reverse of course, a single vote going straight to the bottom.

The popularity is calculated based on TMDB's user interactions, things like page views, users adding it to a list, users rating it, etc. Release date also plays a part with recently released shows weighted higher in popularity.
